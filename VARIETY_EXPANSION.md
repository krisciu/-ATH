## ðŸŽ¯ Design Philosophy: Seeds Not Scripts

All additions are **conceptual frameworks** for the AI, not rigid storylines:

- **Scenarios** provide starting concepts, not full plots
- **Themes** are constraints that guide tone, not rules
- **Endings** are conclusion frameworks, not fixed text
- **Mutations** are reality-bending concepts, not exact effects
- **Visual effects** are tools the system can deploy

The AI has creative freedom to interpret and combine these seeds in infinite ways.
